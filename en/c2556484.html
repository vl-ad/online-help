Working with Search Results

The Search function may return many results. The EdgeRover app helps you narrow down these results to help you find your files much faster.

Results are immediately organized into different categories by file type, such as Photos and Videos.

A filter feature is available to focus on items of specific interest.

  • Source, such as an internal or external storage device.
  • Category, such as Presentations or Spreadsheets.
  • File type, such as PDF.
  • Location, such as London.
  • Tags, such as car or people.
  • Date the content was created.
  • Date the content was last modified.

Once you have files of interest, you can browse them and access the shortlist of files discovered6.

1 To access original files, the source of the files must be connected to your computer. External storage devices must be connected to your computer to access original files. Your computer must be connected to the internet to access original files in your social media and cloud storage accounts.
