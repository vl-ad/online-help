Why is the EdgeRover app using large amounts of internal storage space?

If the catalog is taking up too much internal storage space, you can reduce its size using Manage Sources. Think about what content is most important to catalog.

For example, if you are using a USB Flash Drive to transfer files from one computer to another, it may be less of a priority to have it cataloged than a device used for archiving more significant files.

  • macOS: ~/Library/Application Support/com.wdc.edgerover/restsdk/data
  • Windows: C:\Users\<your user name>\AppData\Roaming\com.wdc.edgerover\restsdk\data

To manage the catalog:

  1. Navigate to Manage Sources.
  2. On the cataloged storage devices, click on the three horizontal dots menu.
  3. To remove the catalog completely, click on Remove Catalog.
  4. To change the folders being cataloged, click on Manage Catalog (this option is only available on Devices).

In addition to removing a device from the catalog altogether, you can review which internal storage folders are cataloged and remove any that are not needed. Examples of this are Applications/Program Files and tmp (temporary) folders.

Note: It may take some additional time for the catalog to be deleted.
