Why has the cataloging process paused?

In order to not impact the operation of other applications on your computer, the EdgeRover app has some inbuilt safeguards:

  1. If your laptop is running on battery power and the battery level drops to less than 30%, the software will stop cataloging to preserve the battery.
  2. The catalog is stored on your internal storage device - cataloging is paused if the amount of space available drops below a certain threshold so that other applications can continue running without problems.
