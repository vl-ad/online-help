Software Overview

The EdgeRover™ app is designed to help you access and manage your data, in addition to managing a range of supported Western Digital® and SanDisk® devices1.

On the content management side, the EdgeRover app allows you to catalog photos, videos, audio, office documents, and more - across both physical and cloud sources. Once cataloged, you can search and browse your content to view high quality previews of your files from one centralized location, even if the source of your original files is not currently connected2. Additionally, the EdgeRover app automatically organizes your content into categories and allows you to create your own collections of files to make it easier to find them when.

Regarding managing devices, the EdgeRover app can be used to change settings on supported WD® and SanDisk® devices, such as WD® My Passport™, WD® My Book™ Duo and SanDisk Extreme PRO® Portable SSD devices3. The features provided depend on the connected device and include, for example, password protecting it, erasing it, turning on/off the LED light, setting the sleep timer, and renaming the device.

1 The EdgeRover app is compatible with many standard USB direct-attached storage devices accessible by your macOS 10.13+ or Windows® 10+ system. Compatibility with devices, social media, and cloud-based platforms may be changed, terminated or interrupted at any time and may vary by country. User account registration may be required.
2 To access original files, the source of the files must be connected to your computer. External storage drives must be connected to your computer to access original files. Social media/cloud accounts must be connected to your computer to access original files from those accounts.
