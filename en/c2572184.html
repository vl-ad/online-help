Manage Devices

In addition to managing your digital content, the EdgeRover app allows you to manage supported WD® and SanDisk® storage devices9.

Under the device icon, there are three horizontal dots. Clicking on these dots opens a menu where you will see the commands available. For most devices, these commands include:

  • Catalog Device – Add the contents of the device to the catalog, or
  • Remove Catalog – Remove contents of the device from the catalog.
  • Eject – Prepare the storage device so that it can be unplugged safely.

If supported by the device, you will see these additional commands:

  • Set Lock – Password protect the contents on the device.
  • Device Settings – Configure the device, such as editing the device's name, turning the LED on/off, setting a sleep timer, et cetera.
  • Erase Device – Format the device.

Tip: It is advisable to give each device a unique name so that when the EdgeRover app informs you that a file is on a device, you know which one is referenced.

1 The EdgeRover app is compatible with many standard USB direct-attached storage devices accessible by your macOS 10.13+ or Windows® 10+ system. Compatibility with devices, social media, and cloud-based platforms may be changed, terminated or interrupted at any time and may vary by country. User account registration may be required.
