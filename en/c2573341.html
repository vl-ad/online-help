Can I search and browse my content when the storage device is not connected?

Yes! In addition to virtually organizing your content, the EdgeRover app creates a catalog with high-quality previews of the files from your storage devices. With this catalog, you can search and browse the previews of your files, even when your storage device is not connected to your computer. If you need to access the original files, you will need to connect the storage device to your computer15.

1 To access original files, the source of the files must be connected to your computer. External storage devices must be connected to your computer to access original files. Your computer must be connected to the internet to access original files in your social media and cloud storage accounts.
