Single Item View

When you browse or search your cataloged content, you will see small thumbnails of your files. Clicking on a thumbnail opens a larger preview of the file, which is displayed in a Single Item View window, where you can perform many actions.

For additional assistance and information, click on the More Info option, located in the top-right corner of the window. Here, you will see a list of information, such as file name, type, size, source, last modified, created, and the file location. Additionally, you will also be able to add a description and enter custom tags to the selected file.
