Manage Sources

The Manage Sources tab is designed to let you add, remove or change the sources and folders that are cataloged7:

  • Internal Storage - View, access, and manage internal storage.
  • External Storage - Storage devices connected to your computer.
  • Social Media - Social media accounts.
  • Cloud Storage - Cloud Storage services.

First things first! Let's add sources to the EdgeRover app. To add a new source, follow the steps listed below.

1 To access original files, the source of the files must be connected to your computer. External storage drives must be connected to your computer to access original files. Social media/cloud accounts must be connected to your computer to access original files from those accounts.
