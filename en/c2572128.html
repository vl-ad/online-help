Why are some of my videos and audio files not playing?

The software catalogs your content and extracts previews. You may see a preview of a video but cannot play it. The same is true of audio files. There are two scenarios:

  1. The content needs to be accessible before you can play it. If the content resides on an external storage device that is currently inaccessible (e.g. it is on a USB flash device that is not plugged in), you will see the preview for the file but it will not play until you plug in the device.
  2. The video or audio file may be accessible but not supported in the software. In this case, you should use the Open feature to play it in the default application.
